Tedorigawa Junmai Ookarakuchi
MEIRYU

YOSHIDA SHUZOTEN CO., LTD.

Tedorigawa Junmai Ookarakuchi MEIRYU is an indulgent, extra-dry sake boasting a remarkable +12 SMV. Its flavour profile carries a delicate fragrance reminiscent of floral notes. The brew exhibits a full-bodied, well-balanced vibrancy, and a nutty mid-palate that culminates in an excellent, enduring finish, leaving behind a delightful savory essence.

This brew brings with it a refreshing crispness and coolness, presented in an innovative mint green bottle to enhance the drinking experience. Beyond a mere drink, it's an experience—a sensory journey through layers of flavours, inviting the drinker to savour each nuanced note within the sake.
