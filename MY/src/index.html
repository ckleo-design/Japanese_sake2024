About The Campaign
An elevated pairing informed by experts

Guided by the expertise of the city’s sommeliers, distinguished chefs from restaurants all over Singapore have embarked on a quest to handpick the most exceptional sake to pair with their iconic seafood creations. Prepare to enjoy the truly splendid combination of sake and seafood.
